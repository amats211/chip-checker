How to Use

  • Tap Camera — take a photo of the chip directly
  • AI reads the model number automatically
  • Or type the model number manually
  • Not in database? AI searches the web for chip info
  • Get grade, type, storage and PH price instantly

Grade Scale

A++++ — UFS 3.1 · Pinakamataas
A+++ — UFS 2.1/2.2 · High-end
A++ — eMMC 5.1 · Premium
A+ — eMMC 5.1 · Standard
A- — eMMC 4.5 · Older gen

AI Web Search

When a chip is not in the local database, the app uses AI to search online for manufacturer specs, storage, grade and device compatibility. These results appear with a purple "WEB RESULT" badge.
